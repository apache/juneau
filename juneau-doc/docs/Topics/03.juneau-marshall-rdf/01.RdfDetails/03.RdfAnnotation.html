@Rdf Annotation

The {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf @Rdf} annotation is used to override the behavior of the RDF serializers and parsers on individual bean classes or properties.

  • {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf}
    • {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf#beanUri() beanUri}
    • {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf#collectionFormat() collectionFormat}
    • {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf#namespace() namespace}
    • {@link oaj.jena.annotation.Rdf#prefix() prefix}
