The <a href="/site/apidocs/org/apache/juneau/json/JsonParser.html" target="_blank">JsonParser</a> strictly enforces RFC 8259 JSON:

- Double-quoted strings only (single quotes throw `ParseException`).
- No comments, trailing commas, or unquoted keys.
- JSON fragments (string, numeric, or boolean primitive values).

For JSON5 syntax (single quotes, comments, trailing commas, unquoted keys), use <a href="/site/apidocs/org/apache/juneau/json/Json5Parser.html" target="_blank">Json5Parser</a>.
