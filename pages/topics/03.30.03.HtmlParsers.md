The <a href="/site/apidocs/org/apache/juneau/marshall/html/HtmlParser.html" target="_blank">HtmlParser</a> class is used to parse HTML into POJOs.
They can also parse the contents produced by <a href="/site/apidocs/org/apache/juneau/marshall/html/HtmlDocSerializer.html" target="_blank">HtmlDocSerializer</a>.
