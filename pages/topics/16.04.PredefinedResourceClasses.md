The following classes are reference/demo resources that illustrate common microservice admin patterns. They live in the
unpublished `juneau-microservice-examples` module (package `org.apache.juneau.microservice.examples`), not in the
shipped `juneau-microservice`/`juneau-microservice-jetty` jars, so they aren't a dependency you pull in directly —
treat them as copy-and-adapt samples:
